Delete Your Account

Request complete deletion of your Flip account and data

How to Delete Your Account

To request deletion of your Flip account and all associated data, email us at:

floatsllc313@gmail.com

Include the email address associated with your Flip account in your request.

What Gets Deleted

  • Account credentials — Your Firebase authentication record (Apple/Google sign-in link)
  • Subscription data — Your RevenueCat subscription record (active subscriptions must be cancelled separately through Apple or Google)
  • Analytics data — Any session or usage data linked to your account

What Is Stored Locally (On Your Device)

Your saved recipes, dietary rules, collections, and grocery lists are stored only on your device. To delete this data, uninstall the Flip app from your device.

Timeline

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

Questions?

Contact us at floatsllc313@gmail.com for any questions about your data.
